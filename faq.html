Frequently Asked Questions

Is the community free?

Yes — during the launch phase (3–6 months), everything is free.

When does membership begin?

Membership begins once we have built a stable community foundation.

Is this a religious or spiritual group?

No — Gentlemen’s Guild is a modern, secular community.

Who can join?

Any man who wants to grow, connect, and improve his life.

Where is this based?

Africa — with global access online.

Is this for all ages?

Yes, but most of our content is tailored for men 25–65.
